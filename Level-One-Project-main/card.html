The money app
for everyone

Save, spend, send and invest money across
borders.

Save

Putting money away for the future is smart
but it takes discipline - something humans
don’t have in abundance. We’re using
technology to solve that by automating
saving. You set an amount to save and the Nova
app takes care of the rest, prompt
interest payout included.

Spend

Nova gives you more than one way to pay
easily, including a widely accepted debit
card issued in partnership with Visa, quick
web payments direct from your Kuda
account, and location-based gift cards for
shopping and subscriptions.
